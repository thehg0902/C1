# Niche Playbook: Restaurant
Converts on: menu + reservations + food photography quality.
Direction defaults: palette from cuisine culture (ask before stereotyping);
high-contrast food imagery; elegant serif for upscale, bold sans for
casual. Hero: signature dish or dining-room video. Must-have: menu (HTML,
not only PDF - SEO), reservation CTA (booking flag) or phone-first,
hours, map. Avoid: music, autoplaying sound, PDF-only menus.
Conversion: reserve/call above the fold on mobile.
